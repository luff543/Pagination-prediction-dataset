    Lighting effects illuminate live performances and create stunning and creative visuals using professional stage lighting. These types of lights are ideal for DJs, mobile entertainers, theatrical companies, event rooms, and other forms of entertainment. Some special effects include spot lights used to shine on performers or other parts of a stage. Strobe lighting is another type of effect where lights are flashed to sync up with music or on its own.
