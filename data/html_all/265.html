ABOUT TAYLOR MORRIS

Launched in September 2013 by British designers Hugo Taylor and Charlie Morris, Taylor Morris focuses on a tradition and heritage of classic design reinvented and modernised for today’s most discerning eyewear collectors, enthusiasts and any one who shares our passion for creating the highest quality frames. Taylor Morris Eyewear encapsulates the best of British design imbuing it with a timeless elegance.
