    About Us

    The Music Zoo has been selling the finest new and used guitars since 1994

    Our commitment to personalized service, groundbreaking custom instruments, and low prices have made us the first choice for players around the world. We stock and ship guitars, amps, basses, and parts and accessories from dozens of major and boutique manufacturers. We know that buying a guitar or amp is a very personal decision. You're in the right place! The Music Zoo's staff is legendary for their hands-on knowledge of gear and will be sure that you are 100% satisfied with your experience.

    Visit Our Long Island Guitar Shop

    Our retail music store is located in Farmingdale, NY at 123 Smith Street. Guitar players in and around New York City have long visited our retail location for latest in guitar gear. Visit our showroom to view our Fender, Taylor, Martin, Gibson, and Yamaha branded rooms. Additionally we provide guitar setups and repairs through our dedicated repair department. We are authorized repair service for Martin, Fender, Gibson and Taylor.

    Shop Our Online Guitar Store

    We maintain a full catalog of incredible guitars and gear right on our website. From boutique custom builds, to used and vintage gear, on down to amps, effects, parts, and accessories, you'll find something for everyone online now. Sign up for your online account today and join our mailing list for incredible deals and fast, secure checkout. We ship worldwide, so whether you live in New York, across the country, or around the globe, you can get the same incredible gear and customer service offered in our retail store.

    Financing, Payment Options, Trades, and Consignments

    We have a number of great ways available for you to make your next guitar purchase! Choose Affirm Financing or The Music Zoo Credit Card to make easy monthly payments. If you need time, choose our layaway option. If you have a guitar trade, we'd be happy to know about it and will offer you top dollar for your used equipment. If you have time to wait, our guitar consignment option is a wonderful way to get the most value out of your gear, and your payout can be rolled into a new guitar purchase!

    We've worked closely with Fender, Gibson, Taylor, Martin, and Jackson to make exciting custom guitars to our own specifications

    Nearly everyone on the staff at The Music Zoo is a player, has a band, has a collection, or all of the above. So we have a lot of ideas about taking instruments further than what you'll find in a catalog. Fortunately, our close friendships with these manufacturers allow us to make our dreams a reality. We can help make yours come true, too. Through countless visits to factories around the country, we know just what the manufacturers can do, how they do it, and in many cases, the person actually building it. We tend to get the "good stuff" from a lot of manufacturers because they know we really care about quality.

    Our staff is standing by to answer any questions you may have

    Whether you're walking in from the sidewalk in New York or calling in from overseas, every customer gets the same personal service on which The Music Zoo has built its reputation. Please let us know how we can help you!
