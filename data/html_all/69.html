    About Us

    Learn More >>
    Since 1994 The Music Zoo has been selling the finest new and used guitars the world can build. We are as excited today as the day we opened to receive new Les Pauls from Gibson, or to have a vintage Fender Stratocaster come in on trade. We are honored to have built great relationships with Martin Guitar and Fender Custom Shop that have yielded some incredible custom guitar runs. Advanced players and beginners alike will appreciate our deep selection on brands like Taylor and PRS, as well as the effort and attention to detail our customer service team provides each customer. Our entire online catalog is also available at our Long Island store. Whether you buy online or visit our Farmingdale, New York guitar shop, you'll receive an incredible buying experience.
