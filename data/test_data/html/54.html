READ OUR STORY

Order lighting online at dmlights

Lighting is an extension of your personal taste. Lamps express who you are; someone who loves rural conviviality, vintage design or modern shapes. That’s why you’ll find a vast range of lighting for indoor and outdoor at dmlights. All styles, shapes, sizes and colours are present. There’s a light for everyone.

Enormous range of lighting

With an assortment that stretches from floor recessed light to ceiling light, and from indoor to outdoor, you’re all set with us. Choose from more than 50 quality brands such as Artemide, DeltaLight, Flos, Wever & Ducré and Modular Lighting.

View all brands

Dedicated team

The driving force behind the website lies in our merry group of employees including a technically skilled customer service team which is at your disposal each working day. You fire off the questions, they provide you with an appropriate answer.

Meet our team

Service before and after sale

At dmlights we always aim for the perfect service, from a to z. Our customer service team tries to communicate proactively about your orders as much as possible. If the estimated delivery time of your order will not be met, we will inform you in good time.

Fast, reliable shipments

Our warehouses are crammed with lighting. With many products in stock, we are able to deliver quickly and flexibly. Stocked products can be delivered within 2 or 3 working days (depending on the delivery address).
